Bachelor Degree Programs

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) is a three-year program that prepares students to be IT professionals well-versed on IT applications, including web, database, programming, and networking technologies. Students will have a solid IT foundation and will learn how to plan, install, customize, operate, manage, administer, and maintain Information Technology infrastructures. The program provides a solid foundation in IT, with a background in both software and hardware management, and additional emphasis on strong database management skills.

e-Learning or Oline Learning is available across all Higher Education Programs



Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) is a three-year program that prepares students to be IT professionals and researchers with proficiency in designing and developing computing solutions. It is a general computer science program which focuses on concepts and theories, including algorithmic foundations and the implementation and application of information and computing solutions. The program develops the necessary skills to specify, design, develop, and manage computer-based systems solutions and provide systems level support and development. It fosters strong research and analytical skills; which graduates are expected to use as a solid foundation in their IT career.

e-Learning or Online Learning is available across all Higher Education Programs


Bachelor of Science in Information Systems

The Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS) with Specialization in Project Management is a three-year program that prepares students to be IT professionals with a solid background in both Information Technology and business concepts and applications. Students are trained to be expert in the design and implementation of computing solutions that integrate IT with business processes. This program is right for students who would want to work in a business environment with technical or management positions.

e-Learning or Online Learning is available across all Higher Education Programs



Bachelor of Science in Business Administration/h1>

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA), major in Digital Marketing and Web Analytics, is a three-year program which prepares students for positions of responsibility in marketing, business, or entrepreneurship. It arms prospective executives with the knowledge and disciplines required to effectively lead, organize, and run a business. The program uses an integrated approach among the functional areas of business as well as sensitivity to the business environment.
The Marketing Management program prepares the students to be responsive to the total environment by providing technical skills and competencies in the areas of marketing. Theory is blended with practicum activities to give the students a broad and enriched base for a career in marketing.

e-Learning or Online Learning is available across all Higher Education Programs


Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship

The Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship (BSE) with Specialization in Technopreneurship is a three-year program course is designed to allow students to develop, organize and manage a business venture along with any of its risks in order to make a profit. Through its combination of classroom training and experiential learning, the BS Entrepreneurship program will help aspiring entrepreneurs acquire the skills, values and attitudes that will increase their chances of success.

e-Learning or Online Learning is available across all Higher Education Programs



Bachelor of Science in Office Administration

The Bachelor of Science in Office Administration (BSOA) with Specialization in Digital Productivity Tools is a three-year program that prepares the students for a career in an outcome-focused, technology rich, professional environment. This course familiarizes the students with current techniques in office practice and procedures, developments in office systems and technology, good team-working and management skills and application of the principles of good human relations and communications to prepare them to be key players in day-to-day office operations.

e-Learning or Online Learning is available across all Higher Education Programs
